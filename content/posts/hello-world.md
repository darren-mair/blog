Welcome to my blog! 🎉

After thinking about it for a while, I finally decided to start writing. This blog is powered by [Hugo](https://gohugo.io/) and hosted on [GitHub Pages](https://pages.github.com/), which means it's fast, free, and easy to maintain.

## What to expect

I plan to write about:

- **Software development** – tips, tricks, and things I've learned on the job
- **Open source** – projects I'm contributing to or find interesting
- **Technology** – new tools and trends worth paying attention to
- **Side projects** – experiments and builds I'm working on

## Why Hugo?

Hugo is a static site generator written in Go. It's:

- **Fast** – builds sites in milliseconds
- **Simple** – just write Markdown and let Hugo do the rest
- **Flexible** – great themes and customisation options

Combined with GitHub Pages, it's a zero-cost hosting solution that gets out of the way and lets me focus on writing.

Stay tuned for more posts!
